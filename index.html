ABOUT

Hi! I’m Mia,
Currently creating product prototypes based on users’ research to show SAP customers the unlocked innovative potential they get after implementing SAP technologies.
I’m passionate about end users and accessibility.
Why end users?
We’re all looking at how we can make our lives easier, better, more enjoyable. Understanding the end user mean understanding what moves people, what makes them tick. It’s not about making a product people want to use. It’s about making a product people need to use.
Why accessibility?
I’m hard of hearing and some products on the market treat that as an obstacle. Catering for all abilities is not something “extra” to have; it’s a necessity. When we design for all abilities, we open ourselves to more possibilities and address more needs that we intended; that creates more robust products.
Let’s meet – I’d love to answer any question you might have and connect with fellow like-minded people to discuss our love for design.
